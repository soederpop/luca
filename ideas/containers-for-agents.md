# Containers For Agents Idea

Currently, we have the Luca architecture: containers with features, and clients.  You can build up your own custom containers for specific domains, or specific to your organization, or whatever.

Containers can also operate in different runtimes, e.g. in node, or in the browser.

Containers have their own sort of unique identity as well.  And the node and web containers each have the clients and means to talk to one another.

I would love to develop the idea of an Agent, that is aware it is attached to a container.

I'd love for the agent to be able to learn about e.g. the clients available to it, or the features available, and let it have free creative reign to develop capabilities for itself.
